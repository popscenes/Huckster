About Us

Huckster works closely with a hand picked collection of premium restaurants to give customers the best of Melbourne’s cuisine in the convenience of their own homes and offices.
Our on-demand delivery platform connects customers with local couriers, who deliver food from selected restaurants and stores in Melbourne.
We’re not only passionate about good food but also about leaving the smallest carbon footprint that we can.

Mission Statement

To bring the best food in Melbourne into homes and offices across the city, and to give restaurants new ways to manage demand, adjust pricing and increase their profitability.

Our Team

Huckster is built by a passionate team of engineers, designers, operations and growth specialists, and is headquartered in Melbourne, Australia. The company was co-founded by James Huddle and Anthony Borg in August 2015.
